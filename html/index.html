A Conference for Student Centered Learning

We are thrilled to announce the upcoming event Teach Live: Engage- a professional development opportunity for teachers like no other! This event is dedicated to the theme Teach4Tomorrow, and we invite you to join us in exploring new and innovative ways of student-centered learning. At Teach Live: Engage, you will have the opportunity to witness and experience live classrooms where session leaders demonstrate a content lesson, strategy, or approach to teaching with actual students. Attendees will be able to observe engaged, student-centered learning at work in real classrooms to discover ways to enhance and redesign their teaching practices. Experience the transformative impact of experiential learning first-hand during this two-day event.

Don't miss this opportunity to connect with other passionate educators, share best practices, and take your teaching to the next level. Register today and join us for "Teach Live Engage" - the ultimate professional development experience for teachers!

We look forward to seeing you there!
