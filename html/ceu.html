CEU

Welcome to Teach Live! We are excited to offer an exceptional opportunity for teachers to further their professional development and earn Continuing Education Units (CEUs) through our upcoming event. As an approved provider of CEUs by the Mississippi Association of Independent Schools (MSAIS), our program is designed to help educators enhance their skills and knowledge, as well as meet the requirements for maintaining their teaching credentials.

Our CEU program is a great way for educators to advance their careers while staying current with the latest teaching methodologies and techniques. By participating in our program, teachers will gain valuable insights, network with other professionals, and earn credit towards their professional development.

Here are some of the benefits of our CEU program:

  • Approved Provider: Our CEU program is an approved provider by the Mississippi Association of Independent Schools (MSAIS), ensuring the quality and validity of our program.
  • Enhance Professional Development: Our program is designed to provide teachers with the latest tools, methodologies, and best practices in the education industry, to enhance their professional development.
  • Meet Credential Requirements: Our CEU program is an excellent way for teachers to meet the continuing education requirements for their teaching credentials.
  • Gain Recognition: Teachers who earn CEUs through our program can showcase their commitment to lifelong learning and professional growth, which can lead to greater recognition and advancement opportunities.

At Teach Live, we are dedicated to providing high-quality professional development opportunities for educators. Our CEU program is just one way we support our teachers in their pursuit of excellence. Join us for our upcoming event and take the first step in advancing your teaching career.
